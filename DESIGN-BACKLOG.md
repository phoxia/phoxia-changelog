# Design backlog

These deferred requirements need approved designs and evidence before implementation.

## Shared discovery and feedback

- Design command-palette search and product/version selection across public sites.
- Define translation freshness, missing-translation fallback and stale-content states.
- Define feedback flows, privacy disclosure and standardized cross-site links.

## Final experience audit

- Inventory every public route, screen and clickable flow.
- Audit 1440, 1024, 768, 390 and very narrow layouts.
- Specify accessible drawers and dialogs, including focus trapping and Escape behavior.
- Audit live regions and loading, error, empty, offline and stale states.
- Record remaining assumptions before final visual approval.
